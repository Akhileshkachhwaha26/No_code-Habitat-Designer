Welcome to the Habitat Designer!

This upgraded tool allows you to prototype a habitat for space missions with advanced controls:

  1. Set Mission & Habitat: Configure parameters and create the habitat shell.
  2. Auto-Layout: Click "Generate Layout" for an instant, intelligent design suggestion.
  3. Design Manually: Add objects and use the transform gizmo (M, R, S keys) to perfect your layout.
  4. Undo/Redo: Don't worry about mistakes! Use the ↶ and ↷ buttons or Ctrl+Z/Y to undo or redo actions.
  5. Save & Load: Use the 💾 and 📂 icons to save your progress in the browser and load it back later.
  6. Change Views: Switch between a 3D perspective and a 2D top-down view for precise planning.
